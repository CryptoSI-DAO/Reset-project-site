## 🌟 About Reset

Reset isn't about restriction, punishment, or becoming someone else. It's about **getting stronger, feeling better in your body, and actually enjoying the process**.

Laura's journey started in 2019. After years of struggling with her weight, a moment with her son — who wears hearing aids — sparked a change. She couldn't control how kids might treat him, but she could control how she showed up for him.

**She lost 9½ stone in 10½ months.** No personal trainer. No fancy gym. Just consistency, a calorie deficit, and the mental strength to keep going.

Now she's building Reset to help other women — especially mums — do the same.

## 🎨 Brand

Reset's brand is **warm, strong, real, and empowering** — with a pink, white, and gold colour palette that feels fresh and feminine without being fussy.

| Colour | Hex | Use |
