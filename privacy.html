Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reset Fitness ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Reset Fitness is a personal training and fitness instruction business operated by Laura Brandon, based in Bedfordshire, United Kingdom. For any privacy questions, contact Laura at @ggnpowerwomen on Instagram.

2. What data we collect

We only collect the information necessary to respond to your enquiries and provide our services:

  • Contact form submissions: your name, email address, the service you're interested in, and any message you choose to send.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: your name and email address.
  • Client records (if you become a paying client): fitness goals, progress tracking, and any medical or emergency contact information you provide.

We do not use analytics, advertising cookies, or third-party trackers on this website.

3. Why we use your data

  • To respond to your enquiry — this is our legitimate interest in answering the message you sent us.
  • To send the newsletter — only with your consent. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.
  • To provide training services — including class booking, progress tracking, and tailoring workouts to your goals.

4. Legal basis for processing

  • Consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)) — for newsletter sign-ups and any optional information you provide.
  • Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) — to deliver services you've booked and paid for.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — to respond to contact form enquiries.

5. Who we share data with

We use trusted third-party services to operate. Your data is only shared with these providers as needed, and under their own GDPR-compliant terms:

  • Supabase — database hosting for contact enquiries and client records (EU-hosted).
  • Listmonk — newsletter delivery and subscription management.

We never sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers or social media platforms.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Contact enquiries: up to 12 months after the last interaction.
  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe.
  • Client records: for the duration of your engagement plus 6 years (to meet insurance and tax record obligations).

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing)
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, message Laura on Instagram at @ggnpowerwomen.

8. Cookies

This website does not use cookies. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies on this site.

9. Data security

All data is stored in encrypted databases operated by Supabase (EU region). Access is restricted and access-logged. Newsletter data is stored separately in Listmonk with double opt-in confirmation.

10. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we handle your data, please contact Laura first — we'll do our best to resolve it. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
