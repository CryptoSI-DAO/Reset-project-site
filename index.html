Get In Touch ✨ Laura's Story

This is personal.

Laura wasn't always fit. In fact, for most of her life she wasn't. She was overweight, unhappy, and had tried every diet out there. Nothing stuck — because nothing addressed the mental battle behind it.

Then in 2019, everything changed. Her son was four, wearing hearing aids for glue ear. She realised kids can be cruel — and the easiest thing for them to target is their mum's weight. She couldn't control his hearing aids. But she could control how she showed up for him.

With a one-year-old running around and a four-year-old who needed her, she made a change. She joined Slimming World. She started running — or as she puts it, "a very quick walk to begin with." She couldn't afford a gym, so she did it anyway.

She lost 9 and a half stone in 10 and a half months. And she's kept going since — losing another 1.5 stone through body recomp, building real strength, and becoming the fittest she's ever been.

Reset isn't about restriction, punishment, or becoming someone else. It's about getting stronger, feeling better in your body, and actually enjoying the process. Laura's proof that it works — because she's lived it.
